Helping Hand

Hospital Assistance in Kesses Town

About Us

Helping Hand is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing assistance to hospitals and healthcare facilities in Kesses Town. Our team of dedicated volunteers works tirelessly to support the healthcare professionals and patients in need.

Our Accomplishments

  • Provided medical supplies and equipment to Kesses Town Hospital to improve patient care.
  • Organized health awareness campaigns in the local community to promote preventive healthcare.
  • Supported the training and professional development of healthcare workers in Kesses Town.
  • Facilitated blood donation drives to ensure a steady supply of blood for patients in need.
  • Provided financial assistance to underprivileged patients for medical treatments and procedures.

Get Involved
